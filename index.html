Ben Berger is a first-year Ph.D. student in the Public Policy program at Harvard Kennedy School of Governement. His research interests lie in health policy and innovation.

Prior to attending Harvard, Ben worked as a research assistant at the National Bureau of Economic Research. He graduated magna cum laude from the University of Miami in 2016 with a Bachelor's in Economics and Mathematics.
